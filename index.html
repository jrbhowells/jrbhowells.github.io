About

Hi! I'm James.

I'm a Building Performance Analyst at Demand Logic, which means I spend my time helping the buildings we're surrounded by all the time operate more efficiently and comfortable.

What that means is that I analyse all the data that flows out of the Building Management System, and use it to understand which items of plant are operating optimally, and which ones aren't. I then work with our clients to help them further diagnoise, and then fix, anyting I spot.

I studied Design Engineering at university, and still have a strong interest in mechanical design and robotics - I work as a Teaching Assistant in Robotics at Imperial College London. Current hobby and side projects include Merlin Maps (a personal project providing mapping and planning tool for outdoor adventures), a novel way to present guide book content digitally for the Blue Guides, and any number of smaller practica projects - involving lots of 3D printing, wood- and metalwork.

You can see much of my portfolio on this website, but for a PDF copy or for my CV, please contact me on jrbhowells@gmail.com.

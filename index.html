About

I am currently in my third year studying Design Engineering at Imperial College London.

I am also a Student Expert at my department's student-run workshop and makerspace, which means I help to maintain a 3D printer farm, laser cutter and other pieces of equipment, and teach others how to use them. I'm on the committee of Imperial's Outdoor society, and have also been involved in the technical & backstage side of the Drama Society.
